Chivalry 2

Chivalry 2 is a first person/third person hyper realistic fighting game;Where quick reactions are rewarded and mistakes punished
you will fight amongst multiple real players in a variety of period accurate maps beautifully desinged
chivalry 2 has a hard to master combat system with satisfying results

Medieval Dynasty

Medieval Dynasty 2 is a survival game whereby you collect resources around the open world around you;in order to build up your very own fully functioning medieval villige.
your goal is to get your village to be completely self suficiant from food production,resource collection,money generation,livestock rearing and farming just to name a few examples.
There are villages within the map whereby you can do story quest and side quest to further improve relations and knowledge.

Kingdom Come Deliverance 2

This is the newest most anticipated game to come out recently following the first instalment which was released in 2018.KCD2 is and action RPG focused on the full immersion in this
world with a realistic combat system a consiquence for every choice made;craft your weapons and armour in real time being part of every aspect of the proces, brew you potions learn alchemy in order
in order to get the most benifit out of your brews;traverse a vast world with so many secrects to uncover.
